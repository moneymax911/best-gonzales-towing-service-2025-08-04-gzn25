About Gonzales Towing

Founded in 2010, Gonzales Towing began with a single truck and a vision to provide the most reliable and professional towing service in the area. Through dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction, we've grown to become the region's most trusted towing partner, serving thousands of satisfied customers annually.

Our mission is to deliver peace of mind through exceptional service, cutting-edge equipment, and unwavering commitment to safety. We believe in treating every customer like family, providing honest service at fair prices, and being there when you need us most - 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide comprehensive towing services throughout Gonzales and surrounding areas within a 50-mile radius, available 24/7 for your convenience.

What are your response times?

Our average response time is 30 minutes or less in most service areas. For emergency situations, we prioritize immediate dispatch to ensure quick assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and work with most insurance providers. We can provide detailed receipts for insurance claims.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our team is certified and regularly trained in the latest towing and safety procedures.

What types of vehicles can you tow?

We handle all vehicle types including cars, motorcycles, SUVs, trucks, RVs, and heavy-duty vehicles. Our fleet is equipped with specialized equipment for safe transport.
